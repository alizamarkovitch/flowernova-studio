If I reached out to you

Here's what
it looks like

If I invited you to participate, you're in the right place — here's exactly what it looks like and how to say yes.

If you received a message from me, this page explains what participation involves and how to pick a filming date. It's low-pressure and entirely at your pace.

1
A quick pre-chat
About 10 minutes — to meet, answer questions, and make sure it feels right.
2
We meet and I make you flowers
60–90 minutes together. We talk, I arrange, and we let things unfold naturally.
3
You receive the finished video and photos
Yours to share however feels right — no obligations.
A note on comfort
  • We keep it natural and low-pressure throughout
  • You can skip any question — no explanation needed
  • You choose what to share — light or deeper, it's up to you
  • No pressure to share anything personal — it can be light, simple, and fun.
