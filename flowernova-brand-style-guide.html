01 — Brand Overview

Who we are

FlowerNova Studio is a contemporary floral design practice rooted in connection and play. Every arrangement is crafted by Aliza Markovitch with seasonal blooms, creative intention, and a deep love for bringing people together through beauty.

01
Mission
Contemporary floral design for connection & play
We believe flowers are a language. Each arrangement speaks of care, intention, and the fleeting beauty of living things.
02
Aesthetic
Romantic refinement
The visual identity sits at the intersection of luxury editorial and organic warmth — iridescent, softly moody, deeply intentional.
03
Audience
Thoughtful celebrants & creatives
Couples planning weddings, community organizers hosting gatherings, and anyone who believes in the transformative power of a beautiful room.

07 — Imagery

Photo & visual style

Photography should feel like a moment caught, not staged. Moody but luminous, rich in texture, with flowers always as the emotional centre of the frame.

01
Moody & Luminous
Dark backgrounds that make petals glow. Dramatic contrast, deep shadows, single soft light source.
02
Rich in Texture
Close crops showing stem, petal edge, water droplets. Sensory and tactile — the viewer should feel the flower.
03
Colour Harmony
Images should reference the brand palette — plum tones, dusty lavenders, warm creams. Avoid bright white or neon-saturated shots.
04
Human & Present
When people appear, they are engaged, joyful, and natural. Never posed or stock-photo. Hands in flowers, laughter, community.
