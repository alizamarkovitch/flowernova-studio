About Aliza

The florist
behind FlowerNova

"Guided by Alan Watts' view of work as play, I let each creation unfold naturally, staying present with the process so beauty can bloom in its own time."

I'm Aliza Markovitch, the florist and creative mind behind FlowerNova. My love of flowers began on a farm, where I was reintroduced to them as something alive from the ground, rooted, seasonal, and full of quiet magic. After a decade in digital design, I bring a trained eye for colour, composition, and storytelling to every arrangement.
